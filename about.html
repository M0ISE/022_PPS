Why do I make things?

When I started my degree I thought I was going to be a Front-End developer building pretty websites. But through my degree, I discovered that Design wasn’t just about making things pretty, but also thinking deeply about functionality and the systems behind all that we see and feel. It is about coming to a problem without assuming you know the answer, learning as much as you can and then keep trying till you first the right solution. Bring a design perspective to a problem is this weird tool-weapon-thing, which when wielded properly, well, it'll change the world for the better. But only if we are prepared to do the work that is involved.
